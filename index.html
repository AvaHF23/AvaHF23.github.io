Christmas Gingerbread

- Every Holiday Season, my mother fills our house with the festive smell of gingerbread. She bakes a singular cookie each mornihng so we all wake up to the smell of warm gingerbread.

Filet Mignon

Lemon Bacon Gnocchi

-- Once in fifth grade, my mom did not have enough foods to compile a complete dinner. So instead, she mixed chicken sausage, gnocchi, lemon, bacon, cheese, cream, a splash of white wine, varied erbs and spices, and lastly roasted pine nuts
